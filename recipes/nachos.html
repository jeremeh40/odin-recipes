Nachos Recipe

Description

A Healthy vegetarian Nachos.

Ingredients

  • Corn chips
  • Black beans
  • Tomato can
  • Mexcican spice
  • Garlic
  • Avocado
  • Sour cream
  • Jalepeno

Steps

  1. Cook tomato, garlic, ginger, and onion in pan until soft
  2. Add coconut milk, and spices
  3. Blend until smooth
  4. Add cooked lentils and mix
  5. Serve with bread and rice
